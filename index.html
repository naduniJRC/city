CityScales

Architecture in scale, Experience in place

City Scales is an international architecture workshop series that brings together students, academics, and professionals from universities around the world. Through architectural design, intercultural exchange, and collaboration, participants explore cities at multiple scales and develop innovative responses to real urban challenges.

About the Initiative

City Scales is an international academic architecture workshop. Rather than treating architecture as an isolated object, we read buildings as part of a wider urban system â shaped by streets, public space, infrastructure, ecology, culture, and social life.

Developed through collaboration between partner universities, each edition responds to a real host city and a genuine urban challenge â never an abstract brief. Students move between building, street, neighbourhood, and city scales.

Previous editions have taken place in Colombo, Barcelona, and Shanghai, each offering a distinct urban condition and a unique learning environment that challenges how participants think about the way cities function.

How It Works

The workshop journey

01
Days 1–2

Introduction & Site Immersion

Teams arrive, meet across cultures, and engage with the host city through guided site visits and briefings.

02
Days 3–5

Urban Analysis & Mapping

Structured site analysis producing maps, diagrams, photographic documentation, and multi-scale findings.

03
Days 6–10

Design Development

Studio sessions with international tutors transforming research into architectural proposals.

04
Final Days

Exhibition & Critique

Final proposals presented to invited critics and displayed in a public exhibition.
